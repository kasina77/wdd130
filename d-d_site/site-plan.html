Overview

Purpose

My idea is to create a web page about Dungeons and Dragons. A major hold up for most people trying to get into Dungeons and Dragons is coming up with stories and characters. The website would provide the user with ideas and prompts for adventures and characters. The ideas and prompts could be used as is or the user could expound on them and make them their own, making it easier and more exciting to gather their friends, roll some dice and have fun.

Audience

For all those who wish to go on an adventure with their friends, and need help getting started. A place for people to get ideas and be inspired for adventures and characters. It’s not always easy to come up with ideas for characters so this is a place for anyone who is looking for help with enhancing their experience with Dungeons and Dragons. To get started with an adventure faster and easier with the help of the information on this site.

Content

Home page

Why Come to the Site? My site will be a place where people who are interested in Dungeons and Dragons Can come to get ideas for stories and for ideas for characters they can build and play. Either playable characters(PC) or non- playable characters(NPC). This site will provide ideas that can be used to create a characters backstory as well as personality traits that could be implemented into the characters personality. Not just a site for those wanting to play, but also for those players that wish to run the game themselves as the Dungeon Master or DM. Questions about the Site This site will provide ideas and resources for a DM or anyone to get inspired and get ideas for creating a world full of substance and wonder. To get ideas from anything from lore of a world to simple story ideas that the Dungeon Master can use to create a story for their players.

Things to Do Those who come to the site can expect to come and create character’s or world for Dungeons and Dragons. The site will have enough content so players can mix and match different traits and backstories to make a wide variety of characters to use in their own games.

Everything Necessary The site will provide everything necessary to create characters and worlds. Things like story prompts that will help get a game off the ground that the Dungeon Master can use. Specific personality traits that can be used in the creating of characters. Players and Dungeon Masters will be provided prompts for backstories that can be used for any and all characters to help create fleshed out characters with personality and who are fun to play. Lending itself to create a fun game for all who are present to enjoy. Why this Site? This site will be a friendly and easy to understand site. A site that a view can see the creator cares about what the provided on the site.
