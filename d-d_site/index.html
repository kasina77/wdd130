Why Come to D&D Adventures? This site will be a place where people who are interested in Dungeons and Dragons Can come to get ideas for stories and for ideas for characters they can build and play. Either playable characters(PC) or non- playable characters(NPC). This site will provide ideas that can be used to create a characters backstory as well as personality traits that could be implemented into the characters personality. Not just a site for those wanting to play, but also for those players that wish to run the game themselves as the Dungeon Master or DM. Questions about the Site This site will provide ideas and resources for a DM or anyone to get inspired and get ideas for creating a world full of substance and wonder. To get ideas from anything from lore of a world to simple story ideas that the Dungeon Master can use to create a story for their players.
